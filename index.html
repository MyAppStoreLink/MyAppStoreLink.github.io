How to Request Account Deletion for Talk2Me

We’re sorry to see you go! If you’d like to request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the App: Launch Talk2Me on your device.
  2. Access Settings: Go to the settings or account management section.
  3. Request Account Deletion: Find and select the option for deleting your account.
  4. Confirm Your Request: Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm that you want to delete your account.

What Happens to Your Data?

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Email address
  • User activity logs
  • Saved preferences

Some data may be kept for up to 30 days to ensure that your request is processed correctly. After this period, all personal data will be permanently deleted.
